Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

🔒

Short version: Awekly collects absolutely no personal data. Everything you enter stays on your device. There are no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no third-party tracking of any kind.

1 Overview

Awekly ("the App", "we", "us") is a personal alarm application for Android that replaces generic alarm tones with a Text-to-Speech (TTS) voice message you write yourself.

This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information. Because Awekly is designed to work entirely offline with no cloud services, there is very little to disclose — but we want to be fully transparent about everything the App does and does not do.

2 Information We Collect

We collect no information whatsoever.

Awekly does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, usage data, diagnostic data, or any other information about you or your device.

✓ No account or sign-up required
✓ No analytics or crash reporting
✓ No advertising SDKs
✓ No network requests of any kind
✓ No third-party tracking
✓ No cloud sync or backup

3 Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores alarm data locally on your device only, using Android's standard storage mechanisms:

  • Alarm settings — time, repeat type, wake-up message text, voice gender, and loop preference.
  • App preferences — selected date, UI state.
  • Boot-recovery data — a copy of your alarm list stored in SharedPreferences so the App can reschedule alarms after a device restart.

This data never leaves your device. Uninstalling the App permanently and irreversibly deletes all of it.

4 Text-to-Speech (TTS)

When an alarm fires, the App uses your device's built-in Android TTS engine (such as Google Text-to-Speech or your manufacturer's default engine) to read your wake-up message aloud.

  • The message text is passed to the local TTS engine on-device only.
  • No text is ever sent to Awekly's servers (there are none) or to any external service.
  • Whether Google TTS or another TTS engine transmits data as part of its own operation is governed by that engine's own privacy policy, not Awekly's. Awekly has no control over third-party TTS engines installed on your device.

5 Android Permissions

The App requests only the permissions necessary to schedule and deliver alarms:

PermissionWhy it's needed
POST_NOTIFICATIONSShow the alarm notification on Android 13 and above.
SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM / USE_EXACT_ALARMFire alarms at the precise time you set.
USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENTDisplay the alarm screen over your lock screen when the alarm fires.
WAKE_LOCKKeep the screen on while the alarm is ringing so you can dismiss it.
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETEDAutomatically reschedule alarms after the device restarts.
FOREGROUND_SERVICERun the alarm service reliably in the background.
VIBRATEReserved for future use; not currently active.

None of these permissions are used to access personal data such as contacts, location, microphone, camera, or files.

6 Third-Party Libraries

Awekly uses a small set of open-source libraries solely to render UI and deliver alarms. None of these libraries send any data off your device:

  • react-native-tts — interfaces with the device's local TTS engine.
  • react-native-linear-gradient — renders UI gradients.
  • react-native-svg — renders vector graphics.
  • @notifee/react-native — manages notification channel permissions only; does not track usage.
  • @react-native-async-storage/async-storage — reads and writes local app data.

There are no advertising, analytics, or crash-reporting SDKs included in the App.

7 Children's Privacy

Awekly does not collect any information from anyone, including children. The App is safe for users of all ages and fully complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

8 Data Security

Because no data is transmitted off your device, there is no data in transit to protect and no server to secure. Your alarm data is protected by your device's own security mechanisms (lock screen, encryption, Android's app sandbox).

9 Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this Privacy Policy, we will post the new version at this URL and update the "Last updated" date at the top. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Because we collect no data, any future changes are unlikely to affect your privacy in a material way.
