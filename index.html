Features

Everything you need for a mindful wake-up

Write your message. Pick a voice. Set your time. Awekly does the rest.

🗣️

Text-to-Speech Alarms

Your alarm message is read aloud by your device's built-in TTS engine — slow, clear, and calm. No ringtones, no music, just your words.

✍️

Custom Wake-Up Message

Write anything — a morning affirmation, your first task for the day, or a motivational reminder. The message is completely yours.

🎙️

Male & Female Voice

Choose the voice gender that feels right. Male voice uses a lower pitch (0.8), female a softer higher pitch (1.3). Both speak at a relaxed 0.5× rate.

🔁

Repeat Until Dismissed

Loop mode re-speaks your message every 1.2 seconds until you dismiss — no drifting back to sleep with a half-heard alarm.

📅

One-time, Daily & Weekly

One-time alarms deactivate after firing. Daily alarms repeat every day. Weekly alarms repeat on the same day each week.

😴

5-Minute Snooze

Not quite ready? Snooze schedules a new native alarm exactly 5 minutes out. Dismiss when you're truly awake.

🌅

Dynamic Sky UI

The home screen gradient shifts from deep night blue through sunrise orange to day sky blue to sunset amber — matching the real sky outside your window.

🔄

Boot-Proof Reliability

Reboot your phone and alarms reschedule automatically. No re-setup required after a restart or battery drain.

🔒

Over-Lock-Screen Delivery

The alarm screen appears over your lock screen the moment it fires. Your PIN stays intact — no forced unlock, no delay.

How It Works

Three steps to a better morning

STEP 01
✍️

Write your message

Type exactly what you want to hear the moment you wake up — an affirmation, a task, or a simple reminder.

STEP 02
⏰

Set the time & repeat

Use the circular dial to pick your wake-up time. Choose one-time, daily, or weekly. Select your preferred voice.

STEP 03
🔔

Wake up intentionally

When the alarm fires, your device reads your message aloud — right over the lock screen, as many times as needed.

🔐

Your privacy is absolute

Awekly collects zero data. No accounts, no analytics, no internet connection. Everything you type stays on your device and is deleted permanently when you uninstall the app.
